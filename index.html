Hello!

Hi, my name is Sy Doan and I'm a doctoral student in the department of Leadership, Policy, and Organizations at Peabody College, Vanderbilt University. This page is a link to various tutorials, explainers, and miscellany that I've created. You may notice that these guides cover a random smattering of topics. What's the method to the madness? Largely, these are topics that I found personally challenging and felt the need to explore further.

I made these guides as a personal study tool. Their focus is not on completeness or, sometimes even fidelity, but rather, to draw out concepts that I find important and/or interesting. I've posted them on Github in hope that:

  1. They can serve as useful materials for others
  2. You can correct me via e-mail or carrier pigeon

As such, I make no promises on the usefulness or accuracy of the above material.
